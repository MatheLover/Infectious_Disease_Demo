About

This site allows users to make queries about Malaria and other relevant data. The information are from WHO , Our World in Data, The UNESCO Institute for Statistics (UIS).

Home
In Home, users can query malaria statistics about their chosen country or region during a specified period.

Malaria Statistics Mapping
In Malaria Statistics Mapping Section, users can choose either to view annual(on a specific year) disease statistics or cumulative(during a period of time) disease statistics upon a real map. In particular, a choropleth map with time slider will be generated to visualize changes in disease statistics over the specified period.

Environmental Factor
The amount of rainfall also affects the severity of malaria. This section enables users to explore the influence of rainfall on malaria

Social Economic Factor
Social economic situations are also related to malaria. In this section, users are encouraged to examine the relationship between socio -economic factors and malaria.
